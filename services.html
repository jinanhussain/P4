Services

At P&J Delights, we are thrilled to offer a seamless and personalized experience to turn your sweet dreams into delicious realities. To make the process as smooth as our frosting, here's a guide on how to order our delectable custom desserts:

How to Order

Inquiry Form: Visit our website and fill out our user-friendly inquiry form to provide details about your desired custom dessert. We'll get back to you promptly to discuss the sweet specifics.

Direct Message (Instagram): Slide into our Instagram DMs with your custom dessert vision. We love connecting with our customers on social media, making the ordering process a delight.

Text or Email: For those who prefer a more traditional approach, feel free to text us at [Your Phone Number] or email us at [Your Email Address] with your order details. We're here to accommodate your preferences.

Confirmation and Deposits

Once your order details are received, we'll send you all the information you need to confirm your order. To secure your spot on our baking calendar, a deposit is required. Your order is considered confirmed once the deposit is received.

Pickup Information

Our pickup location is in Teaneck, NJ. Two days before your scheduled pickup, we'll send you the address to ensure a smooth and convenient experience. Please note that the remaining balance is due prior to pickup.

Delivery Options

If you prefer the convenience of delivery, we offer this service for an additional charge. The delivery fee depends on your location, ensuring that your custom desserts arrive safely and on time.

Dessert Stand Rentals

Elevate your dessert presentation with our stylish dessert stands. We offer a variety of stands to suit your aesthetic. Prices vary depending on the stands you choose and the quantity required. Inquire about stand rentals when placing your order.

Final Payment and Address

The remaining payment for your custom desserts is due before pickup. Once the final payment is received, we'll provide you with the pickup address (for orders scheduled for pickup) or coordinate the delivery details.

We are dedicated to making your experience with P&J Delights delightful from start to finish. Reach out, and let's create sweet memories together!
