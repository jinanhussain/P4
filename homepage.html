Happy You’re Here!

Hello there! I'm Jinan, a self-taught baking enthusiast and the creative force behind a delectable world of custom desserts. Currently based in NJ/NY, my culinary creations extend far and wide, reaching the tristate area and beyond. My journey into the sweet realm of baking began as a casual hobby back in 2021, and since then, it has blossomed into a genuine passion that has seamlessly woven its way into the fabric of my life. As a college student with an insatiable love for all things sweet, I've transformed this passion into a flourishing creative business. Specializing in crafting custom desserts for a myriad of occasions, I bring a personal touch to events such as birthdays, graduations, religious holidays, baby showers, and more. Whether you're celebrating a milestone or simply craving a sweet treat, I'm here to turn your vision into a delicious reality.
